Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies or equipment?+
No - our team brings all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer us to use specific products you already own, just mention it in the booking notes.
How is your staff trained and supervised?+
Every team member is carefully selected and trained to hotel housekeeping standards, and works under close supervision on every visit.
What areas do you currently service?+
We currently cover Colombo and the surrounding areas. If you're just outside this range, WhatsApp us and we'll confirm whether we can accommodate you.
Can I reschedule or cancel a booking?+
Yes - just reach out via WhatsApp or call as early as possible and we'll help you find a new time that works.
What if I'm not happy with the clean?+
Let us know within 24 hours and we'll send the team back to make it right, at no extra charge - that's our satisfaction guarantee.
